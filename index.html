End-to-End System Flow

Understanding how each component works together to create a seamless voice interaction

Step 1

User Places Call

The caller dials the system number, initiating the voice interaction through standard telephony infrastructure.

Step 2

Voice Capture & Conversion

The system captures the caller's speech and converts it to text using automatic speech recognition technology.

Step 3

Intent Understanding

AI analyses the transcribed text to understand what the caller wants, extracting key information and context.

Step 4

Response Generation

The language model generates an appropriate, contextual response based on the understood intent and conversation history.

Step 5

Text-to-Voice Conversion

The generated text response is converted into natural-sounding speech using advanced voice synthesis.

Step 6

Voice Playback

The synthesised voice response is played back to the caller, completing the interaction cycle seamlessly.
